Event Information

 

When and where?

January 15 - 17 2021 @ St George's College

GenghisCon 2021 will be returning to St George's College 46 Mounts Bay Rd, Crawley WA 6009.

Car

If you are driving to GenghisCon, there is some off-site parking behind the college. Please reserve all on-site parking for people with disabilities.

Bus

TransPerth buses stop right outside the college. Coming from Perth get off at Mounts Bay Rd After Crawley Av.

Eat

St George's College meal tickets are available for purchase before the con, and a limited number will be available at the registration desk during the con. Please see the map for additional places to eat in Nedlands.

Stay

Accommodation is available on-site at St Georges College. Please download and complete the booking form obtained below and return it to the email provided on the form.

Download accommodation booking form

Covid-19

What happens if the con doesn't run in 2021?

If GenghisCon needs to be cancelled because of government or health recommendations, all tickets brought through grenadine will be refunded on request or honoured as 2022 tickets. If you would like a refund, please email your ticket name to info@genghiscon.org. Unfortunately, we cannot guarantee a refund for accommodation or meal tickets. These refunds are at the discretion of St Georges College.

If we need to cancel the con, the AGM will be held online, and information will be provided in advance.
