Covid-19

What happens if the con doesn't run in 2021?

If GenghisCon needs to be cancelled because of government or health recommendations, all tickets brought through grenadine will be refunded on request or honoured as 2022 tickets. If you would like a refund, please email your ticket name to info@genghiscon.org.

If we need to cancel the con, the AGM will be held online, and information will be provided in advance.
