What happens if the con doesn't run in 2022?

If GenghisCon needs to be cancelled because of government or health recommendations, all tickets brought through grenadine will be refunded on request via email to genghisconperth@gmail.com (provide your name), or honoured as 2022 tickets. Unfortunately, we cannot guarantee a refund for accommodation or meal tickets. These refunds are at the discretion of St Georges College.

If we need to cancel the con, the AGM will be held online, and information will be provided in advance.
